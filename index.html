Overview

I am now a postdoctoral researcher at University of Pennsylvania, hosted by Prof. Weijie Su. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Princeton University, hosted by Prof. Jason D. Lee. Before that, I received my PhD at Peking Univerity in 2019, advised by Prof. Zhouchen Lin. I also closely work with Prof. Tong Zhang.

My research interests include machine learning algorithms and theory. I currently focus on the foundation of deep learning and various aspects of optimization.

I am recruiting self-motivated interns who have strong mathematical abilities or coding skills to work with me. If you are interested, please send your detailed CV to my email.
